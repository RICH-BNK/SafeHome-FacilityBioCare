Pre‑Inspection Checklist

Use this checklist to prepare your property before our free inspection – and to stay rodent‑free.

Clear clutter in basements, attics, and storage rooms
Check for rodent droppings, gnaw marks, or nesting materials
Inspect walls, floors, and foundations for holes or cracks
Store food in sealed containers and dispose of garbage regularly
Trim vegetation and remove debris near building exterior
Fix leaking pipes and eliminate standing water sources
Seal gaps around pipes, vents, and utility lines (steel wool + caulk)
Keep garbage bins tightly closed and away from building entry
Note any unusual smells or signs of biohazard (urine, feces, carcasses)

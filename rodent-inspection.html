Professional Rodent Inspection

Identifying risks before they become infestations

What does a thorough rodent inspection include?

Our certified inspectors use advanced tools and years of experience to detect every sign of rodent activity – even hidden nests inside walls or ceilings.

  • Visual inspection of all accessible areas (attic, basement, crawl spaces, kitchen, store rooms)
  • Droppings, urine stains, and gnaw mark identification
  • Entry point detection (gaps around pipes, vents, foundations)
  • Moisture and food source assessment
  • Thermal imaging for hidden nesting areas
  • Detailed report with photos and recommendations

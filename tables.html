Zuri International Foundation

Zuri International Foundation stands as a pillar of hope for teenage mothers who found themselves burdened with parenthood after enduring sexual violence. Many of these young women carry not only children but trauma, isolation, and a lack of family support.

Our mission is to walk with them—to provide shelter, healing, education, and love. We believe in second chances, in the strength of young women, and in a future where no survivor walks alone. Through care, community, and empowerment, Zuri helps these mothers and their children rediscover dignity and joy.
